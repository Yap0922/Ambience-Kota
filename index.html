Ambience Residence

@ Kota Syahbandar

Ambience Residence @ Kota Syahbandar is strategically located in the heart of Melaka, surrounded by lush greenery and the tranquil flow of the river. This picturesque setting provides the perfect backdrop for a relaxing and comfortable lifestyle.

Offering both affordability and luxury, Ambience Residence provides a serene escape with stunning views of the paddy fields and natural landscapes. The development features 24-hour security and sheltered parking, ensuring residents' peace of mind.

Residents can indulge in a wide array of amenities, including an Olympic-sized swimming pool, a jogging track, a fully equipped gymnasium, and a children's playground. Additionally, a convenience store on-site ensures that everyday necessities are always within reach.

Experience the perfect blend of nature and modern living at Ambience Residence @ Kota Syahbandar.
